Safety Policy

TrueWash Laundromart Safety Policy

Introduction

TrueWash Laundromart is comitted to providing a safe and secure environment for our customers and employees. We have implemented this safety policy to help prevent accident and injuries

General Safety Rules

  • Please follow all instructions Posted on machines and signs throughout the Laundromart.
  • Do not overload Machines.
  • Do not leave children unattended to in the Laundromart.
  • Report any hazards or unsafe conditions to a staff member immediately.

Machine Safety

  • Do not use machines that are malfunctioning or appear damaged
  • Do not touch the inside of a washing machine while it is in operation.
  • Do not leave flammable materials near the machines.
  • Keep children away from machines while they are in operation.

Chemical Safety

  • Read and follow all instructions on laundry detergents and fabric softners.
  • Do not mix chemicals.
  • Keep chemicals out of reach of childrens.

Fire Safety

  • Do not smoke or use open flames inside the laundromart.
  • Do mot use appliances that are not approved for use in the laundromart.
  • Report any fires immediately to a staff member and call 0800377757077

Training

All staff members will be trained on the safety policy and procedures. Thos training will include:

  • Fire safety
  • Chemical Safety
  • Machine Safety
  • Emergency Procedures

Employees Safety

Employess must wear appropirate personal protective equipment (PPE) when handling chemiclas.

  • Employees must be trained on the safe operation of all machines.
  • Employees must report any unsafe conditions to their supervisor immediately.

Enforcement

TrueWash laundromart will take appropirate disciplinary action againist any employee who violates the saftey policy.

Contact information

If you have any questions or concerns about safety, please contact a staff member or call 080379057079

Thank you for your cooperation in making TrueWash Laundromart a safe and enjoyable place for everyone.
